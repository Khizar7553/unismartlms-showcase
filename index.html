🤖 Three Innovative AI Agents

📚 Agent 1: Smart Library AI (RAG Engine)

  • Self-contained RAG engine with Hybrid Search
  • Formula: (Semantic × 0.35) + (BM25 × 0.30) + (Keyword × 0.25) + (Structure × 0.10)
  • Upload PDF/DOCX/PPTX/TXT → Ask Questions → Get Answers with Sources & Confidence Scores
  • 384-dimension custom embeddings (no external APIs)

🎤 Agent 2: AI Avatar (Action Agent)

  • Voice-Controlled Action Agent with 70+ Database-Driven Intents
  • Intent Detection Flow: Voice → Intent → Role Check → Redirect
  • "create university" → /University/Create (Admin) | "issue book" → /Issuance/Create (Librarian)
  • Speech-to-Text + Text-to-Speech (Web Speech API)

📊 Agent 3: Research Assistant

  • AI Paper Analyzer for academic research papers
  • Automatic Section Detection: Abstract → Methodology → Results → Conclusion → References
  • Extract key findings, citations, and research gaps
  • Compare methodologies across multiple papers
